Our Story

Established in 2023, Janki Fire and Safety Service is a trusted name in fire protection and safety solutions. In a short span, we have built a strong reputation for delivering reliable and high-quality fire safety services across various sectors, including industries, schools, hospitals, corporate offices, and petrol pumps.

Our services include fire extinguisher refilling, hydro testing, installation, on-site safety surveys, and fire safety training. We are dedicated to ensuring the highest level of safety compliance and protection for our clients by offering cost-effective and professional fire safety solutions.
